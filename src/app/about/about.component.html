About

I was born in Lebanon, a small country on the Mediterranean. I was first introduced to computers at the age of 5 when my brother bought a Pentium 2 computer. As I grew, I started to have an interest in how computers work and how they are built. In 10th grade, I started diving into robotics and electronics, took software classes and formed a background in the field. I chose to go abroad and study at Penn State because I wanted to broaden my horizons and get access to the numerous opportunities available in this wonderful university. I have worked at small and big companies back home and at Penn State since then. I have also participated in many competitions in Penn State and made achievements I am very proud of. Having acquired a strong background in software development, I am now getting focusing on fours areas: C++, Angular Web Development, UX/UI Design and Game Development. In the future, I aim to lead the initiative in influencing other peoples lives in a positive way.
